Learning and Development Specialist

Job Description:

As a Learning and development specialist you need to teach students computer science in a fun and in an interactive way.


Location: Damoh, MP

ROLE & RESPONSIBILITIES:
  • Guiding students with their doubts and lesson plan over multiple communication channels.
  • Soliciting insight and feedback from our content creators, curators, teachers & learners on the floor.
  • Understand the learning deficiencies while interacting with the students and put them to use while creating content.
  • Conducting offline session for student across the district.

THE INDIVIDUAL:
  • Good communication skills to interact cross-functionally and/or with seniors, with an ability to convey messages in a clear and structured manner
  • Attention to detail
  • Great intuition for how students like to learn and insight into their common misconceptions.
  • Have discretion and an ability to maintain confidentiality
  • Drive and commitment to deliver high quality service
  • Ability to work well in teams
  • Understanding of IT systems, knowledge of Computer science
  • Have the ability to work under pressure
  • Demonstrate integrity, values, principles, and work ethic

What we expect of you:
  • Should be proficient in English & Hindi/Local language
  • Eager to teach students offline.
  • Highly energetic and self-motivated
  • Eager to solve problems and help thousands of students with their academic doubts. Make learning fun!
  • Familiarity with common curricula and teaching methods.
  • Should be able to work in a team
  • A degree in computer science would be beneficial.
