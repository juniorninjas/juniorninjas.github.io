Objectives

An introduction to computer science for beginners. The objective of this course is to introduce the student to the world of computer science, principles of programming and getting connected to the internet. Students will use a visual block platform(scratch) to learn the basics of coding and work through fun challenges! Visual Block programming have a visual interface and consists of dragging and dropping blocks of instructions to be executed in order. It is a great way to build a logical thinking foundation such as creating sequence, conditional statements, loops, functions, and debugging. The kids are able to create programs or instructions to the computer without learning the syntax of a computer language. In this course we would analyze the interest of children in computer science through various techniques. Keep in mind though that every child is different. Some kids take a larger amount of time to learn the concepts while others who are naturally inclined to computational thinking breeze through learning even at a young age. Coding is not just about building softwares and applications, it helps children with communication, creativity, math, problem-solving, etc.

Eligibility

Grade 6 to 10 students are eligible for this course.
